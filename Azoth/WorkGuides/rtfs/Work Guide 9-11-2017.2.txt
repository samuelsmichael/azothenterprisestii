<topractice>Each day, take a few minutes to sit quietly and gently place attention on your breath. Without interfering, try to be aware of how the lungs invite the air in naturally, 
air rich in cosmic substances. As the body releases the air, try to sense these cosmic substances continuing to penetrate within you, while remembering why you work.</topractice>

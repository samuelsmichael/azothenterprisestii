<toreadpages>Section 83 Can Being Change? - pp.179-181</toreadpages>
<topractice>Take a familiar object (such as a pen, plant, watch, dish, keys, coins, etc.) and place it on the table before you. 
Look at it. What do you see? Now close your eyes for 10 seconds and reject the previous impression. Open your eyes and look again. 
Now what do you see? Repeat this several more times, each time rejecting the previous image. Try to see past your associations. 
Perhaps the object may begin to become unfamiliar.</topractice>

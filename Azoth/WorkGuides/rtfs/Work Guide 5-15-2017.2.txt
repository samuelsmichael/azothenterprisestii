<topractice>When the body is at rest (sitting, standing or lying down), just prior to any movement (getting up, walking, etc.), try to be aware of where the movement comes from within you. This exercise, such as when getting up from a meal, a sitting or resuming a momentum that has been paused, should be done intentionally, not passively.</topractice>

<ponderence>Man lives in life under the law of accident and under two kinds of influences again governed by accident. The first are influences created in life or by life itself. The second are influences created outside this life, influences of the inner circle or esoteric influences &ndash; created under different laws, although also on the earth.</ponderence>

<topractice>During the course of your day, at various times, an attention appears that recognizes the identification of the part at that moment. Instead of having this attention swept into 
some &ldquo;doing&rdquo; to fix the identification, try to remain conscious of your mechanicality and &ldquo;be&rdquo; with it. 
Sustain this effort of attention for as long as you can, and try to Self Remember that the part is not your real &ldquo;I&rdquo;.</topractice>
